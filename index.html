- About -

Hi! I'm Jessica Jang.

I'm a web developer & designer living and working in Portland, OR. I synthesize both form and function to create useful, beautiful websites for local businesses. My passion is to help small businesses reach a larger, more inclusive audience through an increased web presence.

When I'm not researching, planning, or developing a new project, you can usually find me reading or out for a hike. Occasionally, you can even find me out on the golf course with my husband. I'm an early bird who tries to take advantage of the day as much as I can - especially those precious, creative morning hours.

Thanks for visiting my site.
